Our Story & Team

Who We Are

We exist to design deep cultural connections through travel

Founded in 2018, Nomad Horizon Travels was born from a simple observation: conventional tourism often skips the local narratives in favor of standardized routes. We wanted to build a travel collective that prioritizes local heritage, micro-experiences, and genuine luxury.

We design every itinerary from a blank canvas. Our travel designers collaborate with local historians, chefs, and family artisans to ensure your journey is unique, sustainable, and respectful of local cultures.

01.

Deep immersion

We choose slow travel over checkbox tours. We want you to understand the spirit, food, and people of the places you visit.

02.

Seamless Luxury

From private airport transfers to boutique accommodations and five-star dinners, comfort is never compromised.

03.

Local Sustainability

We work directly with local families, boutiques, and conservation efforts to make sure travel dollars stay in the host communities.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are international flights included in the package?

Our featured packages generally exclude international airfare to and from your home country, giving you the flexibility to choose your preferred airline and class of travel. However, once you arrive, all internal flights, rail tickets, and ground transfers specified in the itinerary are fully covered.

Can I customize the destinations and itineraries shown?

Absolutely! The packages listed on our Destinations page serve as curated recommendations. Every trip can be completely tailored. You can adjust the trip duration, upgrade accommodations, add specific tours, or change activities. Our travel designers will work with you to create your perfect itinerary.

What is your cancellation and booking deposit policy?

To secure a custom booking, we require a 25% non-refundable deposit. The remaining balance is due 60 days before your departure. If you cancel more than 60 days before the trip, you forfeit only the deposit. Cancellations made between 30 and 59 days before departure are subject to a 50% cancellation fee, and cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable.

Do you recommend or require travel insurance?

We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance for all our guests. This insurance should cover trip cancellation, travel delays, medical emergencies, evacuation costs, and luggage loss. We can recommend top travel insurance providers during the booking process to ensure you are fully protected.
